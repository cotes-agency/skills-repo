# Business Strategy

## Overview
This skill contains the comprehensive strategic approach for designing, validating, and documenting a Micro-Agency business. 

Depending on what you are trying to achieve, refer to the following specialized guides:

### Business Model Canvas
For designing, filling, and stress-testing the core mechanics of how your business creates, delivers, and captures value. Includes Micro-Agency specific adaptations like the "Time & Energy" block for the founding team.

👉 See [business-model-canvas.md](references/business-model-canvas.md) for the complete guide.

### Business Plan
For structuring and writing a formal action plan. Perfect for synthesizing market research, competitive positioning, operational planning, and financial projections into a cohesive document.

👉 See [business-plan.md](references/business-plan.md) for the complete guide.
